Privacy Policy - Schedlix

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in the Schedlix app. Effective Date: July 9, 2026.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect or store your personal data on our own servers. Your work shifts, notes, settings, and other app data are stored locally on your device. Third-party services used for subscription verification and advertising (such as RevenueCat and Google AdMob) may process only the technical information necessary to provide their services, in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

To verify and manage subscriptions, the app utilizes RevenueCat, Apple App Store, and Google Play. These services may process technical data required for processing and verifying purchases in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

The free version of the app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect technical information, such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device identifiers, and other technical information to serve and measure personalized ads based on your interests and usage of our app or other sites and apps. Depending on your region (e.g., EEA and UK), you will be asked for explicit consent before personalized ads are displayed, and you can manage your preferences at any time. For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses data.

5. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes the locally stored app data from your device. If your data has been included in an operating system backup (such as iCloud Backup or Google Drive), it may be restored if that backup is restored.

6. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Changes are effective immediately upon publication.
